# Achievement Bank is the single source of truth

CV/résumé content lives in one canonical **Achievement Bank**; the website and every tailored **CV Variant** are *views* (selections) over it, never separate copies.

Chosen over a separate CV-only store to avoid content drift and make per-listing tailoring coherent — a corrected fact or sharpened metric is edited once and propagates to the site and every CV.

**Considered:** a standalone CV system (e.g. Reactive Resume) alongside the site — rejected because it duplicates content in two places and lets the site and CV tell different stories.

**Consequence:** the website's content now flows through a selection/tagging layer instead of being hand-written inline; slightly more build indirection, accepted as the cost of one source of truth.
